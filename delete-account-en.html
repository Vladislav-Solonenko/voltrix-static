How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your Voltrix account at any time through the app. Account deletion is an irreversible action.

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Open the Voltrix app on your device
  2. Log in to your account (if you are not already logged in)
  3. Go to the "Profile" section (profile icon in the menu)
  4. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section
  5. Tap the "Delete Account" button
  6. Confirm the deletion in the dialog box that appears
  7. After confirmation, your account will be deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile (phone number, name, email)
  • Powerbank rental history
  • Linked bank cards
  • Payment and transaction history
  • App settings
  • Authentication tokens
  • All personal data associated with the account

What Data May Be Retained

In accordance with legal requirements, some data may be retained for a certain period:

  • Financial data and transaction history ‚Äî up to 5 years (tax law requirements)
  • System logs and technical data ‚Äî up to 30 days (for security and troubleshooting purposes)
  • Anonymized statistical data ‚Äî may be stored in aggregated form without the ability to identify the user

Processing Time

Account deletion occurs immediately after confirmation in the app. All data is deleted within 24 hours of confirmation.

Account Recovery

After account deletion, recovery is not possible. If you want to use the Voltrix service again, you will need to create a new account with a new phone number.
